What's your Outdoor Vibe?

We are a platform that shares off the beaten path and fun hikes in the state of California. By catagorizing the activities in to half-day or overnight, you will be able to quickly see what hikes near you are suited for your outside activity vibe. Wheather you are looking to enjoy a couple of hours hike to a hot spring, or a backpacking trip through Santa Anita Canyon, we have you covered.

Half-Day Locations

Start by 10AM

This includes locations for day use and Hikes. The hiking trails range from one to ten miles, so be sure you read thoughouly! All the outdoor activities found in this category will leave you back in your car if you start by 10 AM.

Overnight Locations

Will take the whole day or more

This includes locations that require backpacking or more serious equipment for a longer duration. The start or end points can be used for day activities, and you can always choose to do just a portion of the trail before turning around.
